It's a great day to be a Keelhauler

Your journey starts here. Located on a scenic waterfront campus in Vallejo, CA, Cal Maritime is one of seven degree-granting maritime academies in the United States — and the only one on the West Coast.

School of Engineering

Build, Solve, Augment

The mission of the School of Engineering is to provide each cadet with a world class education and experiential training in engineering and applied technology. 

Learn more about the School of Engineering

School of Letters and Sciences

Discover, Create, Cultivate

The mission of the School of Letters and Sciences is to play a formative role in every cadet's baccalaureate degree. As the intellectual foundation of the university, programs encourage intellectual, professional and ethical growth.

Learn more about the School of Letters and Sciences

School of Maritime Transportation, Logistics and Management

Navigate, Broaden, Analyze

The mission of the School of Maritime Transportation, Logistics and Management (MTLM) is to instill cadets with the necessary knowledge, practical skills, judgment and character needed to broaden their horizons and become ethical leaders in the global maritime marketplace.

Learn More about the School of Maritime Transportation, Logistics and Management

The Corp of Cadets

Grow, Lead, Elevate

Since 1929, Cal Maritime has combined the roles of student, leader, athlete, and teammate into what we know today as a cadet. When a cadet graduates from Cal Maritime, they will be ready to step into any professional setting as a leader.

Learn More about the Corps of Cadets
